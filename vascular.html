Vascular

Complete Vascular workflow and revenue cycle management including consultations, diagnostics, procedures, coding, billing, and quality compliance.

Workflow Overview

Referral → Consult → Test → Treat → Procedure → Follow-Up → Code → Bill → Collect

Vascular-Specific Considerations:
  • High medical necessity scrutiny
  • Expensive implants & devices
  • Complex global surgery billing
  • Frequent payer audits
  • Strong coordination with wound care & cardiology
Patient Access & Referral Intake:
  • Referral intake (PCP, cardiology, podiatry, wound care)
  • Appointment scheduling (clinic or procedure)
  • Prior medical records & imaging request
  • New vs established patient identification
  • Insurance information capture
Eligibility & Prior Authorization:
  • Insurance eligibility verification
  • Referral validation
  • Prior authorization for vascular imaging (CTA, MRA), interventional procedures, endovascular surgeries
  • Patient financial responsibility estimation
Vascular Consultation:
  • Detailed history & physical examination
  • Vascular exam (pulses, ABI, Doppler)
  • Risk factor assessment (DM, HTN, smoking)
  • Review of prior imaging & tests
  • Diagnosis & treatment planning
Diagnostic Testing:
  • Non-invasive vascular studies (Duplex ultrasound, ABI/TBI, Segmental pressures)
  • Advanced imaging referrals (CTA / MRA)
  • Lab work (if required)
Treatment Planning & Procedures:
  • Medical management (antiplatelets, statins)
  • Surveillance strategy
  • Interventional vs surgical decision
  • Patient education & informed consent
  • Endovascular procedures: Angiography, Angioplasty & stenting, Thrombectomy
  • Open vascular surgery: Bypass, Endarterectomy, AV fistula creation
  • Implant & device tracking
Pre-Procedure & Post-Procedure Care:
  • Pre-Procedure: Surgical scheduling, medical & anesthesia clearance, pre-op testing & instructions
  • Post-Procedure: Recovery monitoring, access site care, discharge instructions, complication monitoring
Follow-Up & Long-Term Care:
  • Post-procedure clinic visits
  • Surveillance imaging
  • Wound care coordination
  • Risk factor management
  • Return-to-activity guidance
Documentation & Coding:
  • Clinic & operative notes
  • ICD-10 diagnosis coding
  • CPT/HCPCS procedure coding
  • Global period management
  • Modifier usage (-25, -59, -RT/-LT)
Charge Capture:
  • Evaluation & management charges
  • Diagnostic testing charges
  • Procedure & implant charges
  • Same-day posting
Claim Submission:
  • Claim scrubbing & edits
  • Electronic submission (837P / 837I)
  • Timely filing compliance
Payment Posting:
  • ERA/EOB posting
  • Contractual adjustments
  • Global surgery tracking
  • Patient responsibility posting
Denial Management:
  • Authorization denials
  • Medical necessity denials
  • Bundling & modifier issues
  • Appeals & corrected claims
Patient Billing & Collections:
  • Patient statements
  • Payment plans
  • High-cost procedure collections
Quality, Compliance & Reporting:
  • Outcome tracking (limb salvage, patency)
  • Readmission & complication rates
  • A/R & denial analytics
  • Provider productivity
