Neurology

End-to-end neurology revenue cycle management supporting complex diagnostics, procedures, strict payer requirements, and optimized financial performance.

Workflow Overview

Referral → Verify/Auth → Pre-Visit Prep → Check-In → Clinical Encounter → Diagnostics & Procedures → Document & Code → Bill → Collect → Follow-Up

Key Neurology Workflow Challenges:
  • High prior authorization dependency
  • Complex diagnostic testing and procedures
  • Strict medical necessity scrutiny by payers
  • Extensive clinical documentation requirements
Patient Access & Scheduling:
  • Referral intake (primary care, ER, self-referral)
  • Insurance verification & prior authorization
  • New vs established patient scheduling
  • Subspecialty triage
  • Demographics, insurance & referral collection
Pre-Visit Preparation:
  • Medical record retrieval (imaging, labs, prior notes)
  • Pre-visit questionnaires (symptoms, history, meds)
  • Diagnostic testing confirmation (EEG, EMG, MRI)
Clinical Encounter:
  • Comprehensive neurological evaluation
  • Diagnosis and treatment planning
  • Orders for imaging, labs, and procedures
  • Care coordination (PT, pain management, surgery, infusion)
Diagnostic & Procedural Services:
  • EEG, EMG/NCS, sleep studies
  • Botox therapy and infusion services
  • Imaging coordination
  • Results interpretation and follow-up planning
  • Clinical decision-making documentation
Coding & Charge Capture:
  • Complex ICD-10 diagnosis coding
  • CPT & HCPCS coding for E/M & procedures
  • Accurate charge entry and reconciliation
Claims Submission:
  • Claim scrubbing & validation
  • Timely electronic submission
  • Payer-specific neurology policy compliance
Denial Management & AR Follow-Up:
  • Authorization & medical necessity denials
  • Bundling & documentation-related denials
  • Appeals with clinical support
  • Continuous AR monitoring
Patient Billing & Collections:
  • Patient statements & payment plans
  • High-deductible balance support
  • Transparent financial communication
  • Patient counseling & follow-ups
Reporting & Performance Optimization:
  • Clinical productivity & utilization metrics
  • Days in AR, denial rate & net collections
  • Workflow optimization
  • Compliance audits & risk mitigation
