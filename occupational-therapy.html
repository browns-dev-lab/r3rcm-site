Occupational Therapy

End-to-end Occupational Therapy revenue cycle management designed to ensure compliance with POC certification, authorization tracking, and accurate timed CPT billing.

Workflow Overview

Schedule → Verify/Auth → Check-In → Eval → POC Cert → Treat → Re-Eval → Discharge → Code → Bill → Collect

OT-Specific Considerations:
  • MD referral & Plan of Care certification required
  • Visit limits & authorization tracking
  • Timed CPT codes & 8-minute rule compliance
  • Pediatric & cognitive documentation scrutiny
Patient Access & Referral Intake:
  • Appointment scheduling (phone, portal, referral)
  • Referral/script intake (MD, Ortho, Neuro, Pediatrician)
  • Prior records & therapy history request
  • New vs established patient identification
  • Insurance information capture
Eligibility & Authorization:
  • Insurance eligibility verification
  • OT benefits & visit limits validation
  • Prior authorization & POC approval
  • Patient financial responsibility estimation
Patient Check-In:
  • Demographics & insurance verification
  • Consent & HIPAA forms
  • Copay collection
  • Functional outcome questionnaires
Initial Evaluation:
  • Occupational profile (ADLs, IADLs, work/school roles)
  • Physical, cognitive & sensory assessment
  • Pain, ROM, strength & coordination testing
  • Functional limitations identification
  • Clinical impression & goal setting
Plan of Care (POC) Development & Certification:
  • Treatment goals & interventions
  • Frequency & duration
  • Referring provider certification
  • Authorization confirmation
Treatment Sessions:
  • Therapeutic activities
  • Self-care & ADL training
  • Fine motor & coordination therapy
  • Cognitive & sensory integration
  • Patient & caregiver education
Progress Notes & Re-Evaluations:
  • Outcome tracking & goal updates
  • Continued medical necessity documentation
  • Re-certification if required
Assistive Devices & Splinting:
  • Splint fabrication
  • Adaptive equipment training
  • DME documentation & education
Discharge Planning:
  • Goal achievement evaluation
  • Discharge summary
  • Home program & caregiver training
  • Return-to-work/school recommendations
Documentation & Coding:
  • Daily treatment documentation
  • ICD-10 diagnosis coding
  • CPT coding (timed vs untimed)
  • Modifiers (GO, KX, CQ/CO)
  • 8-minute rule compliance
Charge Capture & Claims:
  • Timed-unit calculation
  • Same-day charge posting
  • Electronic claim submission (837P)
  • Timely filing monitoring
Payment Posting & Denials:
  • ERA/EOB posting
  • Authorization & medical necessity denials
  • Timed unit & modifier issues
  • Appeals & corrected claims
Patient Billing & Reporting:
  • Patient statements & payment plans
  • Self-pay package tracking
  • Outcome measures & utilization tracking
  • A/R, denial trends & therapist productivity
