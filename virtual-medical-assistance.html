Virtual Medical Assistant (VMA)

End-to-end virtual clinical and administrative support designed to improve provider efficiency, documentation accuracy, and revenue performance.

R3 RCM’s Virtual Medical Assistant (VMA) services extend your care team by handling critical administrative and clinical support tasks. From patient access and virtual rooming to real-time scribing and post-visit coordination, our VMAs enable providers to focus on patient care while ensuring RCM-ready documentation.

Key Benefits of VMA Workflow
  • Reduces provider burnout
  • Improves documentation accuracy
  • Increases patient throughput
  • Enhances revenue capture
  • Improves patient experience
VMA Workflow Overview
Patient Access & Scheduling
  • Answer inbound calls and portal requests
  • Schedule new, follow-up, and annual visits
  • Update patient demographics
  • Verify insurance eligibility
  • Obtain referrals and authorizations (if required)
  • Send appointment reminders
Pre-Visit Preparation
  • Chart review in EHR
  • Update problem list and past history
  • Medication reconciliation
  • Identify care gaps (AWV, screenings, labs)
  • Preload visit templates
  • Confirm patient readiness
Patient Check-In Support
  • Virtual check-in assistance
  • Confirm insurance and demographics
  • Collect copay information
  • Upload ID and insurance cards
  • Ensure consent forms completion
Clinical Intake (Virtual Rooming)
  • Document chief complaint
  • Capture vitals (RPM or patient-reported)
  • Update allergies
  • Review medications
  • Update social history
  • Prepare provider notes
Provider Support (During Encounter)
  • Real-time or asynchronous medical scribing
  • HPI and ROS documentation
  • Update assessment and plan
  • Place orders (labs, imaging, referrals – per protocol)
  • Close documentation gaps
  • Time-based E/M support
Post-Visit Coordination
  • Prepare After Visit Summary (AVS)
  • Send prescriptions for provider approval
  • Schedule follow-up appointments
  • Referral coordination
  • Patient instructions and education
Documentation & Coding Support
  • Complete encounter notes
  • Assist with CPT and ICD-10 suggestions
  • Apply modifiers (as guided)
  • Ensure documentation compliance
  • Flag coding risks
RCM & Administrative Support
  • Charge capture review
  • Claim-ready documentation
  • Prior authorization follow-ups
  • Eligibility and benefit rechecks
  • Denial prevention support
Continuous Quality & Productivity Improvement
  • Provider time savings tracking
  • Turnaround time monitoring
  • Error reduction and QA audits
  • HIPAA compliance checks
