General Surgery

Comprehensive management of General Surgery workflows and revenue cycle processes, ensuring compliance, accurate coding, and timely reimbursement.

Workflow Overview

Referral → Consult → Auth → Pre-Op → Surgery → Recovery → Follow-Up → Code → Bill → Collect

General Surgery-Specific Considerations:
  • Heavy prior authorization dependency
  • Global surgery billing rules
  • Professional vs facility billing separation
  • Implant & pathology charge accuracy
  • Frequent payer audits
Patient Access & Referral Intake:
  • Referral intake (PCP, ER, specialist)
  • Appointment scheduling (clinic or surgical consult)
  • Prior medical records & imaging request
  • New vs established patient identification
  • Insurance information capture
Eligibility & Prior Authorization:
  • Insurance eligibility verification
  • Referral validation
  • Prior authorization for: Surgical procedures, Imaging & diagnostics, Hospital/ASC services
  • Patient financial responsibility estimation
Surgical Consultation:
  • Detailed history & physical examination
  • Review of imaging, labs & diagnostics
  • Diagnosis & surgical indication
  • Discussion of treatment options (surgical vs conservative)
  • Informed consent discussion
Pre-Operative Planning & Clearance:
  • Surgical scheduling (hospital / ASC)
  • Medical clearance (PCP, cardiology, anesthesia)
  • Pre-op labs, imaging & testing
  • Medication review & instructions
  • Pre-op patient education
Day-of-Surgery Check-In:
  • Patient registration & ID verification
  • Consent confirmation
  • Anesthesia evaluation
  • Surgical site marking
  • Time-out & safety checklist
Intra-Operative Care:
  • Anesthesia administration
  • Surgical procedure performance
  • Implant & supply tracking
  • Operative note documentation
  • Intra-op complication management
Post-Operative Care:
  • PACU recovery monitoring
  • Pain management
  • Discharge instructions
  • Admission or same-day discharge decision
Post-Op Follow-Up:
  • Post-op clinic visits
  • Wound checks & suture removal
  • Pathology review
  • Complication monitoring
  • Return-to-work/activity guidance
Documentation & Coding:
  • Clinic & operative notes
  • ICD-10 diagnosis coding
  • CPT/HCPCS procedure coding
  • Global surgery period management
  • Modifier usage (-25, -59, -RT/-LT)
Charge Capture:
  • Surgical professional fees
  • Facility charges (ASC/hospital)
  • Supplies, implants & pathology
  • Same-day posting
Claim Submission:
  • Claim scrubbing & validation
  • Electronic submission (837P / 837I)
  • Clearinghouse & payer compliance
  • Timely filing tracking
Payment Posting:
  • ERA/EOB posting
  • Contractual adjustments
  • Global period tracking
  • Patient responsibility posting
Denial Management:
  • Authorization denials
  • Medical necessity denials
  • Bundling & global period issues
  • Appeals & corrected claims
Patient Billing & Collections:
  • Patient statements
  • Payment plans
  • Self-pay & high-deductible follow-ups
Quality, Compliance & Reporting:
  • Surgical outcomes & complication rates
  • Length of stay (LOS)
  • Readmission tracking
  • A/R & denial analytics
  • Surgeon productivity
