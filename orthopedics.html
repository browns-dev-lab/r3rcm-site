Orthopedics

We manage the end-to-end workflow and revenue cycle for orthopedic practices, from scheduling to follow-up care.

Workflow Overview

Schedule → Verify/Auth → Check-In → Intake → Provider Eval → Imaging → Treatment/Surgery → Document & Code → Charge → Claim → Payment → Denials → Follow-Up

Patient Access & Appointment Scheduling:
  • Appointment scheduling (phone, portal, referral)
  • Injury vs chronic condition classification
  • New vs established patient identification
  • Insurance & referral intake
  • Imaging & prior records request
Eligibility & Authorization:
  • Insurance eligibility verification
  • Specialist referral validation
  • Prior authorization for Imaging (MRI, CT), Injections, Surgical procedures
  • Patient financial responsibility estimation
Patient Check-In:
  • Patient arrival confirmation
  • Demographics & insurance verification
  • Consent & HIPAA forms
  • Co-pay collection
  • Work comp / auto accident intake (if applicable)
Clinical Intake & Rooming:
  • Vital signs
  • Injury history & mechanism of injury
  • Pain assessment & functional limitations
  • Medication & allergy review
  • Prior surgeries & imaging review
Provider Evaluation:
  • Detailed musculoskeletal exam
  • Range of motion & strength testing
  • Gait/posture analysis
  • Imaging review (X-ray, MRI, CT)
  • Diagnosis & treatment planning
Diagnostic Services:
  • In-house X-rays
  • External imaging referrals
  • Lab orders (if required)
  • DME evaluation (braces, splints, orthotics)
Treatment & Procedures:
  • Conservative management: Physical therapy referral, Medications, Activity modification
  • In-office procedures: Joint injections (corticosteroid, PRP), Aspiration, Fracture care & casting
  • Surgical decision-making
Surgery Workflow (If Applicable):
  • Pre-Op: Surgical authorization, Medical clearance, Pre-op testing, Patient education & consent
  • Intra-Op: Procedure performance, Implant tracking, Operative report documentation
  • Post-Op: Discharge instructions, Pain management, Follow-up scheduling
Documentation & Coding:
  • Complete operative & clinic notes
  • ICD-10 diagnosis coding
  • CPT/HCPCS procedure coding
  • Global period management
  • Modifier usage (-25, -59, -LT/-RT)
Charge Capture:
  • E/M visit levels
  • Procedures & surgeries
  • DME charge capture
  • Supply & implant charges
  • Same-day posting
Claim Submission:
  • Claim scrubbing & edits
  • Electronic claim submission (837P/837I)
  • Clearinghouse rejections resolution
  • Timely filing monitoring
Payment Posting:
  • ERA/EOB posting
  • Insurance payments & adjustments
  • Global surgery tracking
  • Patient responsibility posting
Denial Management:
  • Authorization-related denials
  • Bundling & global period denials
  • Medical necessity denials
  • Appeals & corrected claims
  • Trend analysis
Patient Billing & Collections:
  • Patient statements
  • Payment plans
  • Work comp / liability billing
  • Patient follow-ups
Post-Treatment Follow-Up & Rehab:
  • Post-op visits
  • Physical therapy coordination
  • Progress & outcome tracking
  • Return-to-work documentation
Reporting & Analytics:
  • Surgical volume & outcomes
  • Provider productivity
  • A/R & denial trends
  • Implant & supply cost analysis
  • Quality metrics
