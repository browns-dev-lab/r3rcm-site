Obstetrics & Gynecology (OB/GYN)

Comprehensive OB/GYN workflow and revenue cycle management including prenatal, delivery, gynecology care, coding, billing, and compliance.

Workflow Overview

Schedule → Verify → OB/GYN Visit → Prenatal / Treatment → Delivery / Procedure → Follow-Up → Code → Bill → Collect

OB/GYN-Specific Considerations:
  • Global OB billing rules
  • Maternity benefit verification is critical
  • High coordination with hospitals & labs
  • Mixed preventive & procedural billing
  • Frequent payer audits on global packages
Patient Access & Appointment Scheduling:
  • Appointment scheduling (OB, GYN, fertility, preventive)
  • New vs established patient identification
  • Pregnancy confirmation visits
  • Insurance & maternity benefit intake
Eligibility & Benefits Verification:
  • Insurance eligibility verification
  • Maternity benefit confirmation
  • Global OB package validation
  • Prior authorization (procedures, ultrasounds)
  • Patient financial responsibility estimation
Patient Check-In:
  • Demographics & insurance verification
  • Consent & HIPAA forms
  • Co-pay collection (GYN visits)
  • Prenatal intake forms
Obstetrics:
  • Initial OB Visit: pregnancy dating, risk assessment, OB history (GTPAL), physical exam, baseline labs & ultrasound, prenatal care plan
  • Prenatal Care Visits: routine prenatal monitoring, ultrasounds, labs, high-risk coordination, patient education
  • Labor & Delivery: admission, fetal monitoring, vaginal/C-section, delivery documentation, newborn assessment coordination
  • Postpartum Care: inpatient monitoring, discharge education, follow-up visits, contraception counseling
Gynecology Workflow:
  • Evaluation & Treatment: well-woman exams, Pap/HPV, pelvic exams, abnormal bleeding/pain, menopause management
  • Procedures & Surgery: in-office (colposcopy, biopsy, IUD), surgical (hysterectomy, laparoscopy, D&C), pre/post-op care
Documentation & Coding:
  • OB global care documentation
  • ICD-10 diagnosis coding
  • CPT/HCPCS procedure coding
  • Global OB billing (antepartum, delivery, postpartum)
  • Modifier usage (-25, -59)
Charge Capture:
  • Global OB package charges
  • GYN visit & procedure charges
  • Ultrasound & lab charges
  • Same-day posting
Claim Submission:
  • Claim scrubbing & edits
  • Electronic submission (837P / 837I)
  • Global OB billing compliance
  • Timely filing monitoring
Payment Posting:
  • ERA/EOB posting
  • Insurance payments & adjustments
  • Global OB reconciliation
  • Patient responsibility posting
Denial Management:
  • Global OB denials
  • Medical necessity denials
  • Bundling & modifier issues
  • Appeals & corrected claims
Patient Billing & Collections:
  • Patient statements
  • Payment plans (prenatal packages)
  • Self-pay maternity management
Quality, Compliance & Reporting:
  • Prenatal care quality metrics
  • C-section rate tracking
  • Postpartum follow-up compliance
  • A/R & denial analytics
  • Provider productivity
