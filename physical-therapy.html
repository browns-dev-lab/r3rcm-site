Physical Therapy

Comprehensive management of Physical Therapy workflows and revenue cycle processes, ensuring compliance, accurate coding, and timely reimbursement.

Workflow Overview

Schedule → Verify/Auth → Check-In → Eval → POC Cert → Treat → Re-Eval → Discharge → Code → Bill → Collect

PT-Specific Compliance Points:
  • Referral & POC certification required
  • Strict visit limits & authorization tracking
  • Timed CPT codes & 8-minute rule
  • Frequent payer audits
Patient Access & Referral Intake:
  • Appointment scheduling (phone, portal, referral)
  • Referral/script intake (MD, Ortho, Neuro)
  • Prior medical records request
  • New vs established patient identification
  • Insurance information capture
Eligibility & Authorization:
  • Insurance eligibility verification
  • Visit limit & benefit validation
  • Prior authorization requirement check
  • Plan of care (POC) authorization
  • Patient financial responsibility estimation
Patient Check-In:
  • Demographics & insurance verification
  • Consent & HIPAA forms
  • Co-pay collection
  • Functional outcome questionnaires
Initial Evaluation:
  • Subjective history & pain assessment
  • Objective testing (ROM, strength, gait)
  • Functional limitation assessment
  • Diagnosis & clinical impression
  • Plan of Care (POC) development
Plan of Care Certification:
  • POC documentation
  • Referring provider certification (MD signature)
  • Frequency & duration approval
  • Authorization confirmation
Treatment Sessions:
  • Therapeutic exercises
  • Neuromuscular re-education
  • Manual therapy
  • Modalities (ultrasound, e-stim, heat/ice)
  • Therapeutic activities
  • Patient education & home exercise program
Progress Notes & Re-Evaluations:
  • Periodic progress assessments
  • Goal updates
  • Functional outcome tracking
  • Continued care justification
Discharge Planning:
  • Goal achievement evaluation
  • Discharge summary
  • Home exercise continuation plan
  • Referral back to provider (if needed)
Documentation & Coding:
  • Daily treatment notes
  • ICD-10 diagnosis coding
  • CPT coding (timed vs untimed codes)
  • Modifier usage (GP, KX, CQ/CO if applicable)
  • 8-minute rule compliance
Charge Capture:
  • Timed-unit calculation
  • Visit & procedure charge entry
  • Same-day posting
Claim Submission:
  • Claim scrubbing & edits
  • Electronic submission (837P)
  • Timely filing monitoring
Payment Posting:
  • ERA/EOB posting
  • Insurance payments & adjustments
  • Patient responsibility posting
  • Visit limit tracking
Denial Management:
  • Authorization denials
  • Medical necessity denials
  • Timed unit disputes
  • Appeals & corrected claims
Patient Billing & Collections:
  • Patient statements
  • Payment plans
  • Visit package tracking (self-pay)
Quality & Performance Reporting:
  • Functional outcome measures
  • Visit utilization
  • A/R & denial trends
  • Therapist productivity
