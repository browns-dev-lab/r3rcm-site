Radiology

Complete Radiology workflow and revenue cycle management including imaging order processing, technical/professional billing, coding, and quality compliance.

Workflow Overview

Order → Verify/Auth → Schedule → Image → Read → Report → Code → Bill → Collect

Radiology-Specific Considerations:
  • Heavy prior authorization requirements
  • Professional vs technical component billing
  • Critical results communication compliance
  • Radiation safety regulations
  • High payer audit exposure
Order Entry & Referral Intake:
  • Imaging order receipt (EMR, fax, portal)
  • Order completeness check (diagnosis, modality)
  • Medical necessity validation
  • Prior imaging & history review
  • Insurance information capture
Eligibility & Prior Authorization:
  • Insurance eligibility verification
  • Prior authorization for MRI, CT, PET, advanced imaging
  • Referral & ordering provider validation
  • Patient financial responsibility estimation
Scheduling:
  • Modality-specific scheduling (CT, MRI, X-ray, US)
  • Prep instructions (contrast, fasting)
  • Safety screening (metal, pregnancy)
  • Patient reminders
Patient Check-In:
  • Identity & insurance verification
  • Consent forms
  • Safety questionnaires
  • Co-pay collection
Imaging Preparation & Acquisition:
  • Contrast screening & IV placement
  • Radiation safety protocols
  • Equipment calibration
  • Image capture by technologist
  • Quality check & repeats if needed
  • Protocol adherence & upload to PACS
Image Interpretation & Reporting:
  • Radiologist reads images
  • Comparison with prior studies
  • Impression & findings documentation
  • Critical results communication
  • Report dictation, transcription, sign-off & distribution
Documentation & Coding:
  • ICD-10 diagnosis coding
  • CPT/HCPCS modality coding
  • Contrast & supervision codes
  • Modifier usage (-26, -TC, -59)
Charge Capture:
  • Technical component charges
  • Professional component charges
  • Contrast & supply charges
  • Same-day posting
Claim Submission:
  • Claim scrubbing & edits
  • Electronic submission (837P / 837I)
  • Timely filing monitoring
Payment Posting:
  • ERA/EOB posting
  • Contractual adjustments
  • Patient responsibility posting
Denial Management:
  • Authorization denials
  • Medical necessity denials
  • Bundling & modifier issues
  • Appeals & corrected claims
Patient Billing & Collections:
  • Patient statements
  • Payment plans
  • Self-pay imaging packages
Quality, Compliance & Reporting:
  • Radiation dose monitoring
  • Turnaround time (TAT)
  • Peer review & quality audits
  • A/R & denial analytics
