Pain Management

Comprehensive management of pain management workflows and revenue cycle processes, ensuring compliance, accuracy, and timely reimbursements.

Workflow Overview

Schedule → Verify/Auth → Check-In → Intake → Provider Eval → Procedures/Med Mgmt → Follow-Up → Document & Code → Bill → Collect

Pain Management-Specific Considerations:
  • Heavy prior authorization dependency
  • Strict opioid compliance
  • High-value interventional procedures
  • Imaging & fluoroscopy documentation
  • Frequent payer audits
Patient Access & Referral Intake:
  • Appointment scheduling (phone, portal, referral)
  • Referral intake (PCP, ortho, neuro)
  • Prior medical records & imaging request
  • New vs established patient identification
  • Insurance information capture
Eligibility & Prior Authorization:
  • Insurance eligibility verification
  • Referral validation
  • Prior authorization for: Injections & procedures, Imaging, Implantable devices
  • Patient financial responsibility estimation
Patient Check-In:
  • Demographics & insurance verification
  • Consent & HIPAA forms
  • Co-pay collection
  • Pain questionnaires & intake forms
Clinical Intake & Assessment:
  • Vital signs
  • Pain location, severity & duration
  • Functional impact assessment
  • Medication & allergy review
  • Prior treatments & response review
  • Opioid risk screening tools
Provider Evaluation:
  • Detailed pain history & physical exam
  • Imaging review (MRI, CT, X-ray)
  • Diagnosis & pain source identification
  • Treatment plan discussion
Treatment Planning & Compliance:
  • Conservative vs interventional decision
  • Informed consent for procedures
  • Controlled substance agreements
  • PDMP check
  • Urine drug screening (if applicable)
Procedures & Interventions:
  • Epidural steroid injections, Facet joint injections, Radiofrequency ablation (RFA), Nerve blocks, Spinal cord stimulator trials
  • Fluoroscopy guidance documentation
Medication Management:
  • Prescription management
  • Opioid therapy monitoring
  • Refill & compliance checks
  • Patient education
Post-Procedure Care & Follow-Up:
  • Recovery monitoring
  • Discharge instructions
  • Outcome & pain relief assessment
  • Follow-up scheduling
Documentation & Coding:
  • Complete procedure & visit notes
  • ICD-10 diagnosis coding
  • CPT/HCPCS coding
  • Modifier usage (-25, -50, -RT/-LT)
  • Fluoroscopy documentation compliance
Charge Capture:
  • E/M visit levels
  • Procedure & imaging charges
  • Supply & medication charges
  • Same-day posting
Claim Submission:
  • Claim scrubbing & edits
  • Electronic submission (837P/837I)
  • Clearinghouse rejections resolution
Payment Posting:
  • ERA/EOB posting
  • Insurance payments & adjustments
  • Patient responsibility posting
Denial Management:
  • Authorization-related denials
  • Medical necessity denials
  • Bundling & modifier denials
  • Appeals & corrected claims
Patient Billing & Collections:
  • Patient statements
  • Payment plans
  • Patient follow-ups
Quality, Compliance & Reporting:
  • Opioid compliance audits
  • Procedure outcome tracking
  • A/R & denial trends
  • Provider productivity
