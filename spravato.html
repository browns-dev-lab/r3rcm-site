SPRAVATO®

End-to-end revenue cycle management and REMS-compliant workflow support for SPRAVATO clinics treating Treatment-Resistant Depression (TRD) and MDD with suicidal ideation.

Workflow Overview

Identify → Verify/Auth → Pre-Eval → Schedule → Administer → Monitor → Discharge → Document & Code → Bill → Collect → Track Outcomes

Key Success Factors for SPRAVATO Clinics:
  • REMS compliance at every step
  • Strong prior authorization & benefits verification
  • Accurate time-based documentation
  • Tight coordination between clinical & RCM teams
Patient Identification & Referral:
  • Screening for TRD or MDD with suicidal ideation
  • Referral from PCP or psychiatrist (if applicable)
  • Initial eligibility assessment
Benefits Verification & Authorization:
  • Insurance eligibility verification
  • Prior authorization for SPRAVATO
  • Medical necessity documentation
  • REMS enrollment verification (clinic & patient)
Pre-Treatment Evaluation:
  • Psychiatric evaluation & ICD-10 diagnosis
  • Baseline vitals & depression scales (PHQ-9 / MADRS)
  • Medication reconciliation
  • Informed consent
  • Individualized treatment plan creation
Scheduling & Logistics:
  • Dose scheduling (induction & maintenance)
  • Medication ordering & inventory tracking
  • No-driving & fasting instructions
  • Escort confirmation
Day of Treatment – Check-In:
  • Patient arrival & identity verification
  • Vitals & blood pressure check
  • Consent confirmation
  • Copay collection (if applicable)
SPRAVATO Administration:
  • Supervised intranasal administration
  • Provider or nurse monitoring
  • Time-based documentation
  • REMS-compliant tracking
Observation & Monitoring (≥ 2 Hours):
  • Continuous vitals monitoring
  • Sedation & dissociation assessment
  • Safety & stability documentation
  • Clinical clearance confirmation
Discharge Process:
  • Post-treatment evaluation
  • Discharge clearance
  • Escort verification
  • Post-care instructions
  • Next session scheduling
Documentation & Coding:
  • Complete encounter documentation
  • CPT & HCPCS coding
  • ICD-10 diagnosis validation
  • Time & supervision documentation
  • REMS reporting completion
Charge Capture & Claim Submission:
  • Charge entry & claim scrubbing
  • Medical & pharmacy benefit coordination
  • Electronic claim submission
  • Clearinghouse edits resolution
Payment Posting & Denial Management:
  • ERA / EOB posting
  • Authorization & documentation denial analysis
  • Resubmissions & appeals
  • Underpayment identification
Outcomes Tracking & Follow-Up:
  • Depression scale reassessment
  • Treatment response tracking
  • Maintenance schedule adjustments
  • Provider & quality reporting
