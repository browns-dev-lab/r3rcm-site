Sports Medicine

Comprehensive sports medicine revenue cycle management supporting injury care, rehabilitation coordination, procedural services, and optimized reimbursement.

Workflow Overview

Schedule → Verify/Auth → Check-In → Clinical Eval → Diagnostics → Treatment & Procedures → Rehab → Document & Code → Bill → Collect → Follow-Up

Patient Access & Scheduling:
  • Appointment scheduling (acute injury, chronic pain, post-op rehab)
  • Referral intake (PCP, orthopedics, athletic trainers, schools)
  • Insurance eligibility & benefits verification
  • Prior authorization for MRI, injections & procedures
Patient Check-In:
  • Demographics & insurance confirmation
  • Injury history & intake forms
  • Consent forms
  • Copay & deductible collection
Clinical Evaluation:
  • Vitals & musculoskeletal assessment
  • Sports medicine physician consultation
  • Injury mechanism analysis
  • Functional & range-of-motion evaluation
Diagnostic Services:
  • X-ray
  • Ultrasound
  • MRI coordination
  • Gait & movement analysis (if applicable)
Treatment Planning:
  • Non-surgical management plans
  • Medication & pain management
  • Activity modification & return-to-play planning
  • Bracing & orthotics
Procedures & Interventions:
  • Joint & soft tissue injections
  • PRP & regenerative medicine (if offered)
  • Minor procedures
  • Ultrasound-guided interventions
Rehabilitation Coordination:
  • Physical therapy referrals
  • Occupational therapy coordination
  • Athletic training collaboration
  • Home exercise program management
Documentation & Coding:
  • Detailed clinical documentation
  • ICD-10 injury & condition coding
  • CPT & HCPCS procedure coding
  • Medical necessity validation
Charge Capture & Claim Submission:
  • Charge entry
  • Claim scrubbing & edits
  • Electronic claim submission
  • Compliance checks
Payer Adjudication:
  • Claim status tracking
  • Authorization, bundling & necessity denials
  • Appeals & payer follow-ups
Payment Posting:
  • ERA / EOB posting
  • Patient balance calculation
  • Adjustments & reconciliation
Patient Billing & Collections:
  • Patient statements & online payments
  • Flexible payment plans
  • Accounts receivable follow-up
Follow-Up & Return-to-Play:
  • Progress evaluations
  • Return-to-play clearance documentation
  • Injury prevention education
  • Long-term performance optimization
Reporting & Optimization:
  • KPIs: collection %, AR days, denial trends
  • Provider & procedure analytics
  • Revenue optimization insights
