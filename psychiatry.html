Psychiatry

Comprehensive Psychiatry workflow and revenue cycle management, ensuring compliance, confidentiality, accurate coding, and efficient billing for behavioral health services.

Workflow Overview

Schedule → Verify → Check-In → Eval → Treat → Follow-Up → Document → Code → Bill → Collect

Psychiatry-Specific Considerations:
  • Strict confidentiality & consent
  • Time-based psychotherapy coding
  • Telepsychiatry compliance
  • High no-show risk
  • Frequent payer documentation audits
Patient Access & Referral Intake:
  • Appointment scheduling (phone, portal, telehealth)
  • Referral intake (PCP, therapist, hospital)
  • Intake questionnaires & screening tools
  • New vs established patient identification
  • Insurance information capture
Eligibility & Authorization:
  • Insurance eligibility verification
  • Mental health benefits validation
  • Prior authorization (if required)
  • Telepsychiatry coverage confirmation
  • Patient financial responsibility estimation
Patient Check-In:
  • Demographics & insurance verification
  • Consent & HIPAA forms
  • Telehealth consent (if applicable)
  • Co-pay collection
Initial Psychiatric Evaluation:
  • Chief complaint & psychiatric history
  • Mental status examination (MSE)
  • Substance use & risk assessment
  • Suicide/homicide risk screening
  • Diagnosis (DSM-5 aligned)
Treatment Planning:
  • Medication management plan
  • Psychotherapy recommendations
  • Safety planning (if high risk)
  • Patient education & consent
Medication Management:
  • Prescription initiation or adjustment
  • Side-effect monitoring
  • Controlled substance compliance
  • PDMP checks (if applicable)
Psychotherapy Sessions:
  • Individual, family, or group therapy
  • Evidence-based approaches (CBT, DBT, etc.)
  • Progress monitoring
  • Time-based documentation
Care Coordination:
  • Collaboration with therapists, PCPs
  • Referrals to higher level of care (IOP, PHP, inpatient)
  • Crisis intervention (if needed)
Follow-Up & Monitoring:
  • Symptom tracking & outcome measures
  • Medication adherence monitoring
  • Treatment plan adjustments
  • Relapse prevention planning
Documentation & Coding:
  • Psychiatric evaluation notes
  • ICD-10 diagnosis coding
  • CPT coding (E/M, psychotherapy, add-on codes)
  • Time-based & complexity documentation
  • Modifier usage (-25, -95 for telehealth)
Charge Capture:
  • Evaluation & management services
  • Psychotherapy services
  • Add-on procedure codes
  • Same-day charge posting
Claim Submission:
  • Claim scrubbing & edits
  • Electronic submission (837P)
  • Telehealth billing compliance
Payment Posting:
  • ERA/EOB posting
  • Insurance payments & adjustments
  • Patient responsibility posting
Denial Management:
  • Medical necessity denials
  • Telehealth & documentation denials
  • Time-based coding disputes
  • Appeals & corrected claims
Patient Billing & Collections:
  • Patient statements
  • Payment plans
  • Self-pay & sliding scale management
Quality, Compliance & Reporting:
  • Outcome measurement (PHQ-9, GAD-7)
  • Appointment adherence (no-shows)
  • A/R & denial trends
  • Provider productivity
