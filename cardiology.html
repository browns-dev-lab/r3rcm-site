Cardiology

Comprehensive cardiology revenue cycle management covering diagnostic testing, interventional procedures, compliance, and optimized reimbursement.

Workflow Overview

Schedule → Verify/Auth → Check-In → Clinical Eval → Diagnostics → Treatment → Follow-Up → Document & Code → Bill → Collect

Cardiology-Specific Considerations:
  • High-cost diagnostic and interventional services
  • Strict medical necessity documentation
  • Complex coding and modifier usage
  • Prior authorization requirements
  • Frequent payer audits
Patient Access & Scheduling:
  • Appointment scheduling (new & follow-up)
  • Referral intake (PCP, ER, hospital discharge)
  • Insurance verification & eligibility
  • Prior authorization for echo, stress test, cath, EP studies
Patient Check-In:
  • Demographics & insurance confirmation
  • Consent forms & cardiac history intake
  • Copay & deductible collection
  • Visit type assignment
Clinical Evaluation:
  • Vitals & nursing assessment
  • Cardiologist consultation
  • Chest pain, dyspnea & palpitation review
  • Medication reconciliation
Diagnostic Testing:
  • EKG / ECG
  • Echocardiogram
  • Stress testing
  • Holter & event monitoring
  • Cardiac CT / MRI
  • Lab coordination
Treatment & Procedures:
  • Medication management
  • Interventional procedures (angiography, stents)
  • Electrophysiology procedures
  • Pacemaker & ICD management
  • Lifestyle & risk-factor counseling
Documentation & Coding:
  • SOAP notes & clinical documentation
  • ICD-10 diagnosis coding
  • CPT & HCPCS procedure coding
  • Medical necessity validation
Charge Capture & Claim Submission:
  • Charge entry
  • Claim scrubbing & validation
  • Electronic claim submission
  • NCCI & LCD compliance checks
Payer Adjudication:
  • Claim acceptance & rejection handling
  • Authorization & medical necessity denials
  • Appeals & reconsiderations
Payment Posting:
  • ERA / EOB posting
  • Patient responsibility calculation
  • Contractual adjustments
  • Reconciliation
Patient Billing & Collections:
  • Patient statements
  • Payment plans
  • Follow-ups & reminders
  • AR management
Follow-Up Care:
  • Post-procedure visits
  • Ongoing cardiac monitoring
  • Care coordination with PCPs
  • Preventive cardiology programs
Reporting & Optimization:
  • Collection rate, denial rate & AR days
  • Provider performance reports
  • Compliance & audit readiness
  • Revenue optimization insights
